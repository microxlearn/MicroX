MicroX does not require users to register or submit personal information to access learning resources. However, certain non-personal technical data may be collected automatically, including browser type, device details, approximate location, IP address, visit duration, and page interaction data. This information is collected solely to understand usage patterns and improve educational content delivery.

Collected information is used strictly for website optimization, performance monitoring, academic content improvement, and user experience enhancement. MicroX may use cookies and similar technologies to store user preferences, analyze traffic flow, and measure content engagement. Cookies do not contain personal identification data. Users may disable cookies through browser settings, though certain features of the website may function less efficiently as a result.

These services may use cookies, such as the DoubleClick cookie, to show relevant ads based on visits to this and other websites. Users may manage or disable personalized advertising through Google Ad Settings. MicroX does not directly access or control advertiser data collection.

Children’s Privacy.
