Overview

Plus One (Higher Secondary First Year) is the foundation stage where students begin to explore advanced concepts and build a strong academic base for future studies. It introduces structured learning, subject depth, and practical understanding to support higher education and career planning.

Key highlights:

• Comprehensive syllabus aligned with the Higher Secondary curriculum.
• Stream-wise subjects covering Science, Commerce, and Humanities.
• Focus on conceptual clarity, practical skills, and exam-oriented preparation.
• Study materials, notes, solved examples, for all subjects.
• Student-friendly explanations to support daily learning and revision.
