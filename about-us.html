About MicroX

MicroX is a digital learning platform created to simplify the academic journey of Higher Secondary (HSS) and Undergraduate (UG) students. We focus on providing reliable, well-structured, and easy-to-follow study resources.

Our materials include syllabus breakdowns, class notes, previous-year question papers, model exams, and revision-oriented content—all designed to support both classroom learning and self-study.

MicroX continues to evolve with updated subjects, improved tools, and expanded learning support to help students prepare smarter and achieve more.

Our aim is simple: provide high-quality, accessible learning for everyone.
